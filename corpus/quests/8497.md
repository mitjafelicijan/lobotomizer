Title: Desert Survival Kits
Description: With the arrival of fresh recruits to Cenarion Hold comes the problem of keeping them alive in the desert.Dehydration as well as numerous poisonous creatures present a serious threat to inexperienced volunteers.Gather the supplies necessary for a desert survival kit and bring them to Calandrath in Cenarion Hold.
Objective: Bring 4 Globes of Water, 4 Powerful Anti-Venom and 4 Smoked Desert Dumplings to Calandrath at the inn in Cenarion Hold in Silithus.You must also bring Logistics Task Briefing I in order to complete this quest.
