# Paladin Healer Best in Slot Gear Blackwing Lair & Zul'Gurub - WoW Classic Phase 4

Welcome to Wowhead's Phase 4 Best in Slot Gear list for Holy Paladin Healing in WoW Classic. This guide will list the recommended gear for Holy Paladin Healing from raids, dungeons, PvP, professions, world drops, reputations, and instances.

Best In Slot Gear for Holy Paladin Healer
-----------------------------------------

This is the best possible setup in Phase 4 and will set you up very comfortably to heal in the next raid tier, though not every item here is realistically attainable for most players. Alternative options that are sensitive to this reality are listed in the next sections of this guide. It should also be noted that optimal healer play in Classic Vanilla involves swapping between active trinkets with long cooldowns depending on the situation - namely [![Scrolls of Blinding Light](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_scroll_08.gif) Scrolls of Blinding Light](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19343/scrolls-of-blinding-light), [![Major Recombobulator](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_misc_enggizmos_09.gif) Major Recombobulator](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=18637/major-recombobulator), and [![Zandalarian Hero Charm](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_necklace_13.gif) Zandalarian Hero Charm](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19950/zandalarian-hero-charm).
