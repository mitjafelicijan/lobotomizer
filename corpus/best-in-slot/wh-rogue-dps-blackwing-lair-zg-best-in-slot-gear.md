# Rogue DPS Best in Slot Gear Blackwing Lair & Zul'Gurub - WoW Classic Phase 4

Welcome to Wowhead's Phase 4 Blackwing Lair Best in Slot Gear list for Rogue DPS in WoW Classic. This guide will list the recommended gear for Rogue DPS from raids, dungeons, PvP, professions, world drops, reputations, and instances.

Best In Slot Gear for Rogue DPS Phase 4
---------------------------------------

In Phase 4 of Classic you will want to use four pieces of the rogue [Darkmantle Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=512/darkmantle-armor) set and three pieces of [Bloodfang Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=213/bloodfang-armor). The [Darkmantle Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=512/darkmantle-armor) upgraded dungeon set takes a lot of work to get, but you will use this armor all the way into [Ahn'Qiraj](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone=3428/ahnqiraj) in Phase 5. The eight piece bonus on [Bloodfang Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=213/bloodfang-armor) is good, but definitely not good enough to make it worth equipping the full set. Some individual pieces of [Bloodfang Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=213/bloodfang-armor) are actually worse than the individual pieces of even the tier 1 [Nightslayer Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=204/nightslayer-armor)!  
  
Here are some best in slot lists for rogues in Phase 4, depending on your choice of weapon type and race. These lists exclude the exceptionally difficult to obtain Grand Marshal and High Warlord weapons - those weapons are still better than the options listed here but aren't reasonably obtainable by many players.

### Body Armor for Rogue DPS

#### Head for Rogue DPS

The [Darkmantle Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=512/darkmantle-armor) set is going to be your main focus, and [![Darkmantle Cap](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_helmet_41.gif) Darkmantle Cap](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=22005/darkmantle-cap) is one of the four pieces you will want to use. While [![Bloodfang Hood](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_helmet_41.gif) Bloodfang Hood](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16908/bloodfang-hood) is individually stronger than [![Darkmantle Cap](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_helmet_41.gif) Darkmantle Cap](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=22005/darkmantle-cap), it is not powerful enough to be one of the best non-set items to use.

#### Shoulders for Rogue DPS

In phase 1, [![Nightslayer Shoulder Pads](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_shoulder_25.gif) Nightslayer Shoulder Pads](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16823/nightslayer-shoulder-pads) were the choice as one of the strongest non-set items to use while still keeping our valuable [Darkmantle Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=512/darkmantle-armor) bonuses. In Phase 5 with stronger options available in other slots, this is no longer the case so we swap back to [![Darkmantle Spaulders](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_shoulder_07.gif) Darkmantle Spaulders](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=22008/darkmantle-spaulders) in the shoulders. While [![Bloodfang Spaulders](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_shoulder_23.gif) Bloodfang Spaulders](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16832/bloodfang-spaulders) may look very cool, their stats make them definitely worse than the tier 1 [![Nightslayer Shoulder Pads](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_shoulder_25.gif) Nightslayer Shoulder Pads](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16823/nightslayer-shoulder-pads).  
  
For sword rogues, it would be fine to opt for [![Nightslayer Shoulder Pads](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_shoulder_25.gif) Nightslayer Shoulder Pads](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16823/nightslayer-shoulder-pads) & [![Darkmantle Boots](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_boots_08.gif) Darkmantle Boots](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=22003/darkmantle-boots) instead of the technically-best [![Darkmantle Spaulders](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_shoulder_07.gif) Darkmantle Spaulders](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=22008/darkmantle-spaulders) & [![Boots of the Shadow Flame](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_boots_cloth_05.gif) Boots of the Shadow Flame](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19381/boots-of-the-shadow-flame). If you can't manage to get this bow, or you need to choose between this bow or other drops in [Molten Core](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone=2717/molten-core), you'll do just fine without it and stick with your [![Precisely Calibrated Boomstick](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_weapon_rifle_06.gif) Precisely Calibrated Boomstick](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=2100/precisely-calibrated-boomstick) or [![Blackcrow](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_weapon_crossbow_04.gif) Blackcrow](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=12651/blackcrow) - they aren't too much worse.  
  
**Recommended BiS Scope**: [![Sniper Scope](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_misc_spyglass_02.gif) Sniper Scope](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=10548/sniper-scope)
