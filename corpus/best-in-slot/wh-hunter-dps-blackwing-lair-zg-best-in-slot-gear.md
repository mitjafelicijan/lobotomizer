# Hunter DPS Best in Slot Gear Blackwing Lair & Zul'Gurub - WoW Classic Phase 4

Welcome to Wowhead's Phase 4 Best in Slot Gear list for Hunter DPS in WoW Classic. This guide will list the recommended gear for Hunter DPS from raids, world bosses, dungeons, PvP, professions, world drops, reputations, and instances.

Best In Slot Gear for Hunter DPS in Phase 4
-------------------------------------------

[Field Marshal's Pursuit](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=395/field-marshals-pursuit)/[Warlord's Pursuit](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=396/warlords-pursuit) remain insanely powerful for Phase 4. If you can dedicate the time to reaching Rank 13 it is highly recommended you do so. This gear will be used all the way until [Naxxramas](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone=3456/naxxramas). If you can not dedicate your time to Rank 13 then Rank 10 should be done at bare minimum as this will be much easier to achieve by everyone even casually and will give you your BiS helm/shoulders.   
  
With the removal of the debuff limit, and how many Hunters you have in your raid. [Dragonstalker Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=215/dragonstalker-armor) 8/8 could potentially be an option once all hunters have the full set to maximize the uptime of [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/ability_hunter_snipershot.gif) Expose Weakness](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=23578/expose-weakness).   
  
Below are two examples of gear that would serve as "absolute BiS" for Phase 4. This gear set provides the absolute best situation taking advantage of all gear PvP as well as a non-PvP option.
