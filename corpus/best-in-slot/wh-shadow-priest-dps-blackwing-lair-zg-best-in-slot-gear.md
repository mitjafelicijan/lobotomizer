# Shadow Priest DPS Best in Slot Gear Blackwing Lair & Zul'Gurub - WoW Classic Phase 4

Welcome to Wowhead's Phase 4 Best in Slot Gear list for Shadow Priest DPS in WoW Classic. This guide will list the recommended gear for Shadow Priest DPS from raids, world bosses, dungeons, PvP, professions, world drops, reputations, and instances.

Best In Slot Gear for Shadow Priest DPS Phase 4
-----------------------------------------------

With the addition of [Blackwing Lair](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone=2677/blackwing-lair) and the abundance of Hit available from there, Shadow Priests are able to get incredibly close to Hit cap without making any major sacrifices. This set also utilizes a lot of highly contested items, and some world boss items. Because of this, your gear by the end of the Phase is unlikely to look like this. Luckily, there are a lot of good items to fill slots until you can get the BiS options.  
  
This set opts to omit any gear from Ranking, but does include [![Tome of Shadow Force](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_misc_book_06.gif) Tome of Shadow Force](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19309/tome-of-shadow-force) simply because of how accessible and powerful this item is.  
  
The talents used are not set in stone, primarily Wand Specialization, which can easily be swapped into Unbreakable Will.
