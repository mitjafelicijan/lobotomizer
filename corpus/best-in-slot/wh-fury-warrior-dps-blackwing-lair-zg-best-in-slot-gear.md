Gearing Advice for Warrior DPS
------------------------------

### Weapon Skill

In vanilla WoW, all melee classes must reach **at least 305 weapon skills** for each equipped weapon type in order to reduce their chances to miss attacks against bosses by 3% and reduce the damage penalty from glancing blows (40% of melee swings against bosses, inevitable) by 20%. These benefits from +5 weapon skill result in a **7-10% damage increase** and are your first priority when gearing up a [![Warrior](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_warrior.gif) Warrior](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=1/warrior).  
  
[![Human](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/race_human_male.gif) Human](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/race=1/human)s and [![Orc](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/race_orc_male.gif) Orc](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/race=2/orc)s can reach 305 weapon skill passively thanks to their racial abilities [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/ability_meleedamage.gif) Sword Specialization](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=20597/sword-specialization), [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_hammer_05.gif) Mace Specialization](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=20864/mace-specialization), and [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_axe_02.gif) Axe Specialization](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=20574/axe-specialization), making these two races the best picks to increase your damage output as a [![Warrior](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_warrior.gif) Warrior](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=1/warrior).  
On the other hand, all the other races in the game without an innate weapon skill bonus will need to find said bonus from their gear, with items such as [![Edgemaster's Handguards](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_gauntlets_30.gif) Edgemaster's Handguards](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=14551/edgemasters-handguards) or [![Expert Goldminer's Helmet](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_helmet_18.gif) Expert Goldminer's Helmet](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=9375/expert-goldminers-helmet), trading an armor slot to make up for inferior racial abilities. Thanks to these items, all races can reduce the damage penalty for not being [![Human](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/race_human_male.gif) Human](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/race=1/human) or [![Orc](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/race_orc_male.gif) Orc](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/race=2/orc) from a **7-10% damage loss** down to a **2-5% damage loss** depending on phases and available weapons.

### Hit Cap and Hit Value

Assuming you met the first gearing requirement that is to reach 305 weapon skill for your equipped weapons, the minimum hit chance you need to no longer miss your physical abilities is **6% hit** *(9% hit without weapon skill)*. This is your minimum goal to ensure your abilities will land (barring dodges and parries) but that does not mean additional hit chances past this cap have no value.  
  
Since you are dual-wielding two weapons, your melee swings suffer an additional 19% miss penalty. While this miss penalty can be temporarily ignored while queueing [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/ability_rogue_ambush.gif) Heroic Strike](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=25286/heroic-strike) due to intricate game mechanics under the hood, a Fury [![Warrior](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_warrior.gif) Warrior](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=1/warrior) will still miss ~10% of melee swings on average. Thus, hit chances past the 6% hit cap will still increase your damage output and rage generation up to a sweet spot of 8-10% hit chance depending on phases and available items.

### Helm for Warrior DPS

The expensive [![Lionheart Helm](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_helmet_36.gif) Lionheart Helm](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=12640/lionheart-helm) *— crafted by Blacksmiths with the [rare plans](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=12717) —* is still the BiS helmet for a Fury [![Warrior](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_warrior.gif) Warrior](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=1/warrior) all the way to TBC when it comes to raw damage output. However, due to its lack of stamina, it is common practice to save this helmet only for bosses and use a safer alternative with stamina against trash mobs — such as the [![Warlord's Plate Headpiece](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_helmet_09.gif) Warlord's Plate Headpiece](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16542/warlords-plate-headpiece) or [![Helm of Endless Rage](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_helmet_10.gif) Helm of Endless Rage](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19372/helm-of-endless-rage) — so as not to run the risk of losing your precious world buffs prematurely.   
  
**Recommended BiS Helm Enchant**: [![Lesser Arcanum of Voracity](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_misc_gem_03.gif) Lesser Arcanum of Voracity](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=11645/lesser-arcanum-of-voracity)

The [![Don Julio's Band](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_35.gif) Don Julio's Band](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19325/don-julios-band) is essentially free with the [Alterac Valley](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone=2597/alterac-valley) reputation, and any of [![Master Dragonslayer's Ring](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_41.gif) Master Dragonslayer's Ring](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19384/master-dragonslayers-ring), [![Circle of Applied Force](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_37.gif) Circle of Applied Force](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19432/circle-of-applied-force) or [![Band of Accuria](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_15.gif) Band of Accuria](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=17063/band-of-accuria) will perform about the same as your second ring, with a tiny advantage for [![Circle of Applied Force](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_37.gif) Circle of Applied Force](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19432/circle-of-applied-force) when accounting for the [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/ability_creature_poison_05.gif) Spirit of Zandalar](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24425/spirit-of-zandalar) or [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/spell_magic_magearmor.gif) Blessing of Kings](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=20217/blessing-of-kings) buffs.   
  
The catch-up ring [![Seal of Jin](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_44.gif) Seal of Jin](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19898/seal-of-jin) is a great pick-up if you don't have a second epic ring yet, sitting between [![Don Julio's Band](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_35.gif) Don Julio's Band](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19325/don-julios-band) and [![Painweaver Band](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_18.gif) Painweaver Band](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=13098/painweaver-band). The second ring of the set [![Band of Jin](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_44.gif) Band of Jin](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19925/band-of-jin) however is quite a bit weaker and only worth equipping in replacement of [![Don Julio's Band](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_35.gif) Don Julio's Band](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19325/don-julios-band) when paired with [![Seal of Jin](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_44.gif) Seal of Jin](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19898/seal-of-jin) to unlock the [+30 AP](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=466) set bonus, until you obtain a second epic ring.

### Trinkets for Warrior DPS

The iconic [![Diamond Flask](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_drink_01.gif) Diamond Flask](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=20130/diamond-flask) obtained as a reward from the [![Warrior](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_warrior.gif) Warrior](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=1/warrior) quest [![Voodoo Feathers](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/quest-start.gif) Voodoo Feathers](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/quest=8425/voodoo-feathers) is one of the best trinkets in the game until TBC and a **must-have**, so don't pick the wrong reward when turning in the quest or you will brick your character. It increases your strength by +75 for a full minute and will be your best trinket by a landslide up to [Naxxramas](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone=3456/naxxramas).  
  
The second best trinket is the highly coveted [![Drake Fang Talisman](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_misc_bone_06.gif) Drake Fang Talisman](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19406/drake-fang-talisman). It's a great passive trinket to equip and forget about for an entire raid, or to fall back to when your other, better trinkets are on cooldown in future phases. That being said, it's not as big of a deal as every class discord's propaganda will try to make you believe. For most classes, it's merely a <0.5% DPS upgrade over [![Hand of Justice](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_talisman_01.gif) Hand of Justice](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=11815/hand-of-justice) and not worth all that drama for a vanity item. *My recommendation would be to ignore DFT entirely and be content with HoJ to instead leverage priority on a more impactful item such as **weapons**, [![Chromatic Boots](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_boots_plate_04.gif) Chromatic Boots](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19387/chromatic-boots), [![Onslaught Girdle](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_belt_29.gif) Onslaught Girdle](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19137/onslaught-girdle), [![Quick Strike Ring](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_jewelry_ring_07.gif) Quick Strike Ring](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=18821/quick-strike-ring), or more powerful trinkets in future raids.*

### Main-Hand Weapons for Warrior DPS

[![Human](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/race_human_male.gif) Human](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/race=1/human)s must wield **Swords** or **Maces** to benefit from their racial abilities [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/ability_meleedamage.gif) Sword Specialization](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=20597/sword-specialization) and [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_hammer_05.gif) Mace Specialization](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=20864/mace-specialization), while [![Orc](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/race_orc_male.gif) Orc](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/race=2/orc)s must wield **Axes** to benefit from [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_axe_02.gif) Axe Specialization](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=20574/axe-specialization). Raising your weapon skill with said weapons to 305 instead of the baseline 300 reduces your chances to miss attacks against bosses by 3% and reduces the damage penalty from glancing blows (40% of melee swings against bosses, inevitable) by 20%, resulting in a **7-10% damage increase** for using the proper weapon types.  
  
**All other races** without an innate racial ability to increase their weapon skills will have to use [![Edgemaster's Handguards](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_gauntlets_30.gif) Edgemaster's Handguards](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=14551/edgemasters-handguards) as an alternative to try and bridge the gap with [![Human](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/race_human_male.gif) Human](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/race=1/human) or [![Orc](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/race_orc_male.gif) Orc](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/race=2/orc) [![Warrior](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_warrior.gif) Warrior](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=1/warrior)s. With [![Edgemaster's Handguards](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_gauntlets_30.gif) Edgemaster's Handguards](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=14551/edgemasters-handguards) equipped, you'll want to aim for leftover weapons in low demand in your raid/faction, *i.e. axes if you're in the ![](https://wow.zamimg.com/uploads/guide/images/27798.png) **Alliance** because swords will be highly contested by [![Human](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/race_human_male.gif) Human](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/race=1/human) [![Warrior](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_warrior.gif) Warrior](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=1/warrior)s and [![Rogue](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_rogue.gif) Rogue](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=4/rogue)s, and the opposite for ![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/horde.png) **Horde**.*  
  
As a desperate measure, you could be tempted to wield **Daggers** paired with [![Aged Core Leather Gloves](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_gauntlets_23.gif) Aged Core Leather Gloves](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=18823/aged-core-leather-gloves) or [![Mugger's Belt](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_belt_02.gif) Mugger's Belt](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=18505/muggers-belt) if you can not afford [![Edgemaster's Handguards](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_gauntlets_30.gif) Edgemaster's Handguards](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=14551/edgemasters-handguards). While the single-target damage output of daggers isn't terrible, wielding daggers unfortunately cripples your AoE damage potential with [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/ability_whirlwind.gif) Whirlwind](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=1680/whirlwind) and [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/ability_warrior_cleave.gif) Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=20569/cleave) and makes you inefficient at what a Fury [![Warrior](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_warrior.gif) Warrior](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=1/warrior) does best in raids.
