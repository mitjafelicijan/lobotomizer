# Restoration Druid Healer Best in Slot Gear Blackwing Lair & Zul'Gurub - WoW Classic Phase 4

Welcome to Wowhead's Phase 4 Best in Slot Gear list for Restoration Druid Healing in WoW Classic. This guide will list the recommended gear for Restoration Druid Healing from raids, dungeons, PvP, professions, world drops, reputations, and instances.

Phase 4 Best In Slot Gear for Restoration Druid Healer
------------------------------------------------------

Druid Healer Tier 2 - [Stormrage Raiment](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=214/stormrage-raiment)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Stormrage serves as the second raid set for the [![Druid](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_druid.gif) Druid](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=11/druid) class. This set drops within [Blackwing Lair](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone=2677/blackwing-lair), with the Legs from [Molten Core](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone=2717/molten-core), and and the Helm from [Onyxia's Lair](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone=2159/onyxias-lair), and this gear is designed primarily for healing [![Druid](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/class_druid.gif) Druid](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/class=11/druid)s.   
  
[![Stormrage Chestguard](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_chest_chain_16.gif) Stormrage Chestguard](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16897/stormrage-chestguard), [![Stormrage Handguards](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_gauntlets_25.gif) Stormrage Handguards](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16899/stormrage-handguards) and [![Stormrage Bracers](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_bracer_03.gif) Stormrage Bracers](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16904/stormrage-bracers) are the priority pieces from this set that you can collect, as they are the best items in their respective slots to obtain the 3-set [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/spell_nature_sleep.gif) Meditation](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=21894/meditation).  
  
The [![Stormrage Cover](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_helmet_09.gif) Stormrage Cover](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16900/stormrage-cover) and [![Stormrage Legguards](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/inv_pants_06.gif) Stormrage Legguards](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=16901/stormrage-legguards) are good alternative items for the 3-set if you haven't been able to get the Chest, Bracers or Gloves. These two pieces are also the best next options to reach the 5-set [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/spell_nature_magicimmunity.gif) Faster Regrowth Cast](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=21872/faster-regrowth-cast), for druids who prefer to play the [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/spell_nature_resistnature.gif) Regrowth](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=9858/regrowth) build.  
  
The Restoration Druid may wish to equip some Hit items to reduce the chance that their [![](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/wow/icons/tiny/spell_nature_faeriefire.gif) Faerie Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=9907/faerie-fire) will resist in a Raid environment. The Hit options rewarded from Dungeons and Raids are generally highly contested and should be prioritized to Caster DPS first.
