# Balance Druid DPS Best in Slot Gear Blackwing Lair & Zul'Gurub - WoW Classic Phase 4

Welcome to Wowhead's Phase 4 Blackwing Lair Best in Slot Gear list for Balance Druid DPS in WoW Classic. This guide will list the recommended gear for Balance Druid DPS from raids, dungeons, PvP, professions, world drops, reputations, and instances.

Phase 4 Best In Slot Gear for Balance Druid DPS in WoW Classic
--------------------------------------------------------------

There are quite a few upgrades in Phase 4 from Blackwing Lair but most of them will be highly contested. Unfortunately, this is made worse by the fact that the [Stormrage Raiment](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item-set=214/stormrage-raiment) set is only tailored towards healing with both healing stats and healing set bonuses. There won't be any case where you would want to pick these items up unless you were planning on healing.  
  
Items that roll "of Arcane Wrath" are particularly powerful for Balance Druids but can be frustrating to seek out. There are often even green quality items with "of Arcane Wrath" that can be competitive. Keep an eye out on the Auction House for such items as they can be especially worth while early on.  
  
The new Bloodvine set is definitely worth it in this phase, especially if you can make use of the full set bonuses.
